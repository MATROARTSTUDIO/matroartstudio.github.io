About Us

Delhi's Premier
Event Architects

Matro Art Studio is a full-service event planning and management company based in Lajpat Nagar, New Delhi. Established in 2008, we have built a strong reputation for flawless execution and meticulous attention to detail.

Trusted by over 100+ Ministries and Government institutions, our skilled team delivers comprehensive event services — from creative theme conceptualization to full on-ground management. We handle every detail so you can focus on what matters most.
