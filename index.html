Corporate Event Management
We specialize in organizing high-level corporate events for ministries, PSUs, and private sector clients. Our team handles everything — from concept development, logistics, and on-site coordination to post-event reporting. We've successfully managed inter-ministerial conferences, national summits, and award ceremonies for the Government of India.
Exhibition & Display
Creative design and fabrication of exhibition stalls, thematic displays, and product showcases. We deliver end-to-end execution from concept visualization to installation, ensuring every display communicates your brand message powerfully and memorably.
Audio-Visual Solutions
Professional AV setup including LED screens, projection systems, PA systems, stage lighting, live streaming, and video production. We ensure flawless technical execution for conferences of all scales — from boardroom meetings to 1000+ delegate national conferences.
Printing Services
High-quality printing for all event materials including backdrops, roll-up banners, flex printing, fabric displays, directional signage, name plates, table cards, and all collateral required for a professional event environment.
Conference Material
Complete conference kit production and management: delegate bags, branded stationery, agenda booklets, speaker biographies, promotional material, certificates, and awards. We ensure every attendee has a premium experience from the moment they arrive.
Venue Selection & Contract
Expert venue scouting across Delhi's premier event spaces including Vigyan Bhawan, The Ashok Hotel, Manekshaw Centre, Parliament House, and 5-star hotels. We handle all negotiations, contracts, and venue logistics to secure the perfect setting for your event.

Corporate Event Management Company Trusted by Over 100+ Ministries

MATRO ART STUDIO is an event planning and management company, primarily based in Lajpat Nagar, Delhi, that focuses on delivering memorable and customized experiences. Established in 2008, the company leverages a team of skilled professionals to offer comprehensive event services.

Our notable specialization is in Event Management for Government Ministries and Departments. Our service portfolio spans various events, in addition to providing essential services such as creative design, theme conceptualization, venue selection, audio-visual management, signage, and conference material services.

MATRO ART STUDIO aims to flawlessly execute all aspects of an event, ensuring a high level of attention to detail for a wide range of client needs. We have had the privilege of managing events for India's most prestigious institutions, including the Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of Health, and many more.

At Matro Art Studio, we turn corporate events into unforgettable experiences. Our team handles everything from planning to execution, delivering events that inspire, engage, and impress.
